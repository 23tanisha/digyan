What is job responsibility of Telecaller, BPO or Call Center jobs?

The job responsibilities of Telecaller, BPO, and Call Center jobs can vary based on the industry, but here are the common duties:

1. Telecaller Job Responsibilities:

Making outbound calls to customers or leads
Handling inbound customer queries
Explaining products/services and answering customer doubts
Persuading customers to take action (buy, book, or register)
Maintaining call records and updating CRM

2. BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) Job Responsibilities:

Handling customer queries via calls, emails, or chats
Resolving customer complaints efficiently
Following scripts or protocols for customer interactions
Managing data entry and backend operations
Adhering to quality and compliance guidelines

3. Call Center Job Responsibilities:

Managing high-volume calls professionally
Providing technical support or customer assistance
Upselling or cross-selling products/services
Handling escalations and resolving issues
Meeting daily/weekly call targets

These roles demand good communication skills, patience, and problem-solving ability.

What qualification is required for a Telecaller, BPO or Call Center jobs? 

Educational Qualification:
Minimum 12th pass (Higher Secondary) or Graduate (preferred)
Some companies may require a Bachelor’s degree for specialized roles

Skills Required:
Good Communication Skills (Hindi & English preferred)
Basic Computer Knowledge (Typing, CRM tools, MS Office)
Convincing & Persuasion Skills (For sales roles)
Patience & Listening Skills (For customer support roles)
Problem-Solving Ability (Handling customer queries efficiently)

Experience:
Fresher-friendly (Many companies hire freshers)
1-2 years of experience (Preferred for senior roles)
